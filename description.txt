-=- Boss Leaderboards -=-

This is a multiplayer mod that adds a leaderboard for tracking various stats during boss fights.
The leaderboard can be opened by using the "Show leaderboard" keybind (L by default).
The currently tracked stats are:
- Damage
- Kills
- Life Lost
- Hits Taken
- Deaths
